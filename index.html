Weather Analysis

The purpose of this project is to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the ecuator.

To accomplish this, I used the OpenWeatherMap API and citypy to take the cities randomly.

The scatter plots at the side showcase the relationship between:

  • Latitude vs Cloudiness
  • Latitude vs Humidity
  • Latitude vs Wind Speed
  • Latitude vs Temperature
Feel free to click on each plot to have a closer look.

Weather data is from February 14th, 2021
