Latitude vs Cloudiness

The plot shows that latitude has no correlation to cloudiness.

This means that we cannot predict or expect a sunny day only knowing the lattitude of a place.

The following plots help us see that for the Northern Hemisphere (cities above the ecuator) there is a weaker correlation between cloudiness and latitude than in the Southern Hemisphere. But also, we have to consider that there are less cities in this part of the world.
